Privacy Policy (CortiCoach)

Effective date: 2 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CortiCoach (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects information.

1) Who we are

CortiCoach is provided by Aashik Singh (“we”, “us”, “our”).
Support: aashiksingh@gmail.com

2) What the app does

CortiCoach helps you understand a stress trend estimate and provides short lessons and habits that may help you feel calmer. The App may optionally read certain Apple Health (HealthKit) metrics to improve its estimates.

3) Data we collect
A) Health data (optional)

If you connect Apple Health, we may read limited HealthKit data such as:

Heart rate variability (HRV)

Resting/overnight heart rate

Sleep metrics (where available)

You control these permissions in iOS and can revoke access at any time.

Important: CortiCoach does not write health data back to Apple Health unless you explicitly enable a write feature. If the app does not provide a write feature, it does not write to HealthKit.

B) App activity data (stored on your device)

To make the app work, we store some information locally on your device, such as:

Lesson completions, streak, and XP

Your check-ins (how you felt before/after lessons, if you use them)

App settings (for example: notifications on/off)

The app’s locally calculated scores and timestamps

This information is stored on-device (for example using SwiftData / local storage).

C) Purchases and subscription data

If you purchase a subscription or lifetime access, Apple processes payment. We use RevenueCat to power subscriptions and entitlements (for example, to check whether Pro is active, restore purchases, and show subscription management screens).

RevenueCat may process purchase-related information such as:

Subscription or entitlement status (active/inactive)

Product identifiers

App user identifier used for subscription management

Receipt validation results

We do not receive your full payment details (like your credit card number). Those are handled by Apple.

D) Notifications

If you enable notifications, CortiCoach may schedule local notifications on your device. Local notifications are generated by the app on your phone (not sent from our servers).

4) What we do not do

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not run third-party advertising trackers.

We do not store your HealthKit data on our servers (local-first design), unless we clearly tell you otherwise in the future and you opt in.

5) How we use information

We use data only to:

Calculate and display your stress trend estimate and related insights

Power lessons, streaks, and progress

Provide app features you request (like notifications)

Manage purchases, subscriptions, and access to Pro features

6) Sharing

We do not share your health or app data with third parties except:

Service providers needed to run purchases/subscriptions, such as RevenueCat and Apple’s in-app purchase system

7) Data retention and deletion

Because CortiCoach is designed to store core data on your device:

Uninstalling the app typically removes local app data from that device.

You can revoke Health permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

If you need help deleting or resetting data inside the app, contact aashiksingh@gmail.com.

8) Your choices and controls

You can:

Turn Apple Health permissions on/off in iOS Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices

Turn notifications on/off in iOS Settings

Manage your subscription via Apple ID settings (see Section 10)

Settings > Your name > Subscriptions > CortiCoach > Cancel Subscription.
